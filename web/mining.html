How Speedboosts Work

Each mining slot can have one Speedboost NFT staked to reduce mining time. The boost applies multiplicatively to the base mining duration.

  • Rusty Minecart: 6.25% speed boost
  • Greased Minecart: 12.5% speed boost
  • Refined Minecart: 25% speed boost
  • Golden Express: 100% speed boost (2x speed)
  • Arcane Minecart: 50% speed boost
